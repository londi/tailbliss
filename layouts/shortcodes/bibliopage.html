Your digital bookshelf. With Biblio, you always have an overview of the books in your collection, wherever you are.

With Biblio, you can easily and conveniently record both the books you already have and the books you want to buy in the future. Scan the barcode on the back of the book, for example, and off you go!

Biblio can also help you find out whether you already have a certain book. For example, if you are in the bookshop and are not sure whether you already have all the volumes in a series.

Try it out right away. Biblio is free, was developed in Switzerland and runs on a Swiss server.

I wish you lots of fun and friends with Biblio. If you have any questions, suggestions or ideas for new functions, please feel free to write to me.
