Your Digital Bookshelf with Biblio

With Biblio, manage your personal library effortlessly, no matter where you are. This app allows you to conveniently catalog the books you own and create a wishlist for those you'd like to purchase. Simply scan the barcode on the back of a book to add it to your collection.

Ever find yourself in a bookstore wondering if you already own a specific book or if you've completed a series? Biblio can quickly confirm what's on your shelf, preventing any duplicate purchases.
